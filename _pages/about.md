## Introduction
黄虎，中国科学技术大学网络空间安全学院副研究员，长期从事网络认知态势感知与应用相关研究，对网络社会立场形成与演化有较为深入的了解。先后主持和承担国家及省部级科研项目10余项，其中主持国家重点研发计划项目1项，课题2项；在人工智能领域顶级会议和期刊上发表CCF-A类等论文30篇，担任中国计算机学会大数据专委会执委、中文信息学会社会媒体处理专委委员、中国网络空间安全学会制度建设工委会委员等学术兼职，承担ACL、IJCAI、AAAI、CVPR等会议程序委员会委员和Computer Science Review、ACM TOPS、ACM TALLIP、IEEE IoT-J期刊审稿人等学术服务工作。授权国家发明专利15项，相关成果在中宣部、中央网信办等多个部委应用，取得重要的经济社会价值，获得中国电子学会科技进步一等奖等省部级科技奖励2项，其他国家及省部级社会奖励3次。

Hu Huang is an Associate Research Fellow at the School of Cyber Science and Technology, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). His long-term research focuses on cyber cognitive situation awareness and its applications, with in-depth expertise in the formation and evolution of stances in online societies.<br>
He has led and participated in more than 10 national and provincial/ministerial-level scientific research projects, including 1 project and 2 topics under the National Key Research and Development Program of China as the Principal Investigator (PI). He has published 30 papers, including China Computer Federation (CCF) Class A papers, in top-tier conferences and journals in the field of artificial intelligence.<br>
His academic appointments include: Executive Committee Member of the Big Data Special Committee of the CCF; Committee Member of the Social Media Processing Special Committee of the Chinese Information Processing Society of China (CIPS); Committee Member of the Institutional Construction Working Committee of the Cyberspace Security Association of China (CSAC). He has also undertaken extensive academic services, serving as a Program Committee (PC) Member for top international conferences including ACL, IJCAI, AAAI, and CVPR, and as a peer reviewer for journals including Computer Science Review, ACM Transactions on Performance Evaluation of Computer Systems (TOPS), ACM Transactions on Asian and Low-Resource Language Information Processing (TALLIP), and IEEE Internet of Things Journal (IoT-J).<br>
He has been granted 15 national invention patents. His relevant research outcomes have been deployed and applied by multiple national ministries and commissions, including the Publicity Department of the Communist Party of China Central Committee and the Cyberspace Administration of China (CAC), delivering significant economic and social benefits. He has received 2 provincial and ministerial-level scientific and technological awards, including the First Prize of Science and Technology Progress Award from the Chinese Institute of Electronics (CIE), as well as 3 other national and provincial/ministerial-level social awards.
